# 🤖 Market Intel Cad Bot 

## 📌 Overview
**Market Intel Cad Bot** is an AI-powered Telegram trading assistant designed to streamline complex market research, quantitative analysis, and macroeconomic monitoring. Built utilizing **Claude Code**, this bot acts as a centralized intelligence hub that synthesizes global economic data, on-chain metrics, and technical setups into actionable insights.

As a Business Administration student with a deep interest in data-driven digital marketing and financial markets, I developed this tool to bridge the gap between raw macroeconomic data and practical market execution, drastically reducing manual research time.

## ✨ Key Features
The bot is categorized into three main operational pillars:

### 1. Market Analysis & Scanners 📊
*   `/fast` — Delivers instant market signals based on current conditions.
*   `/high` — Scans for high-probability technical setups.
*   `/quant` — Executes quantitative analysis, momentum screening, and statistical reporting.
*   `/quant reversion` — Runs a mean reversion scan to identify overextended assets.
*   `/onchain` — Provides deep on-chain metrics including MVRV, TVL, and Fear & Greed Index.
*   `/liq` — Tracks futures market liquidations and Long/Short ratios.

### 2. Macroeconomics & Sentiment Intel 📰
*   `/crypto` — Analyzes the direct impact of global economic news on the market.
*   `/news` — Fetches the latest crypto and global market news.
*   `/trend` — Conducts social media trend and sentiment analysis.

### 3. Real-Time Alerts & Performance Tracking 🚨
*   **Alerts:** Monitor specific high-impact events (e.g., `/crypto alert NFP BTC`), auto-monitor upcoming events within a 2-hour window (`/crypto auto`), and manage active trackers (`/crypto active`, `/crypto stop`).
*   **Performance:** A built-in trading journal. Users can log trade outcomes (`/result`), view pending setups (`/pending`), and calculate overall win rates and statistics (`/stats`).

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
*   **Core AI Integration:** Claude Code / Anthropic API
*   **Platform:** Telegram Bot API
*   **Data Sources:** Binance API, CoinGecko, Google RSS, Yahoo Finance

## 💡 Why I Built This
In the fast-paced financial market, executing high-quality trades requires analyzing multiple data variables—from global macroeconomic news to micro-level on-chain data. Manually tracking these takes hours. 

This bot was engineered to automate the intelligence-gathering process. By leveraging AI, it translates complex datasets into a digestible format instantly, allowing for rapid, data-driven decision-making and disciplined performance tracking.
